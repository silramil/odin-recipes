Chocolate Chip Cookies

This is a chocolate chip recipe I pulled from Betty Crocker's website.

It's supposed to be the ultimate recipe for gooey chocolate chip cookies.

Ingredients

  • 2.25 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 0.5 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 0.75 cup granulated sugar
  • 0.75 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
